# 🗺️ Strategy Map: EcovodaLife

This map synchronizes **Marketing Intent $\rightarrow$ Content Strategy $\rightarrow$ Visual Execution**. It ensures that every page speaks to the right persona with the right emotional trigger.

## 🎯 Core Brand Promise
*"Medical-Grade Trust meets Nature's Purity"*

---

## 🏗️ The 5 Semantic Hubs

### Hub 1: Safety (Безопасность и здоровье)
- **Emotional Goal:** Relief $\rightarrow$ Security $\rightarrow$ Protection.
- **Target Persona:** **The Protector** (Parents, health-conscious heads of household).
- **Visual Trigger:** 
    - Dominant "Medical White" and "Pure Blue".
    - Heavy use of TrustBar (Certificates) and Comparison Tables.
    - Clean, sterile, high-contrast layouts.
- **Content Source:** `agents/researcher/ecovodalife/content/hub1_safety/pillar_en.md`
- **Key Message:** "Your water is not as safe as you think, but we provide the absolute shield."

### Hub 2: Technology (Технологии)
- **Emotional Goal:** Curiosity $\rightarrow$ Understanding $\rightarrow$ Technical Trust.
- **Target Persona:** **The Solver / Tech-Enthusiast**.
- **Visual Trigger:**
    - Blueprint-style diagrams, technical schematics.
    - Deep Ocean gradients.
    - Precision-focused typography (Sans-serif dominant).
- **Content Source:** `agents/researcher/ecovodalife/data/research/semantic_core/hub2_tech/`
- **Key Message:** "Science-led filtration that leaves nothing to chance."

### Hub 3: Wellness (Велнес)
- **Emotional Goal:** Desire $\rightarrow$ Aspiration $\rightarrow$ Vitality.
- **Target Persona:** **The Optimizer** (Biohackers, longevity seekers).
- **Visual Trigger:**
    - Soft, organic gradients (Teal $\rightarrow$ Light Blue).
    - Imagery of mineral crystals and cellular health.
    - Introduction of premium Serif fonts for aspirational quotes.
- **Content Source:** `agents/researcher/ecovodalife/data/research/semantic_core/hub3_wellness/`
- **Key Message:** "Pure water is the foundation of cellular longevity and peak performance."

### Hub 4: Eco (Экология)
- **Emotional Goal:** Guilt $\rightarrow$ Alignment $\rightarrow$ Empowerment.
- **Target Persona:** **The Sustainer** (Zero-waste advocates, eco-conscious).
- **Visual Trigger:**
    - Nature-integrated visuals, soft green accents.
    - Focus on "Plastic-Free" icons and sustainability metrics.
    - Airy, open layouts with plenty of whitespace.
- **Content Source:** `agents/researcher/ecovodalife/data/research/semantic_core/hub4_eco/`
- **Key Message:** "The most luxurious choice is the one that protects the planet."

### Hub 5: Practical (Практика)
- **Emotional Goal:** Confusion $\rightarrow$ Clarity $\rightarrow$ Confidence.
- **Target Persona:** **The Solver / Pragmatist**.
- **Visual Trigger:**
    - Structured grids, checklists, and step-by-step guides.
    - High-utility icons (Tools, Maintenance, Time).
    - Direct, no-nonsense layout.
- **Content Source:** `agents/researcher/ecovodalife/data/research/semantic_core/hub5_practical/`
- **Key Message:** "High-end technology made simple for your everyday home."
