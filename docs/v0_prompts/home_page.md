PROMPT FOR HOME PAGE:

Create a high-conversion, luxury landing page for "EcovodaLife", a premium water filtration brand. 
Brand Promise: "Medical-Grade Trust meets Natures Purity.

The design must be a Semantic Machine structured around 5 psychological hubs, transitioning the user from fear/desire to technical trust and practical action.

1. Overall Style: 
- Modern, minimal, high-end. 
- Typography: Mix of high-precision sans-serif (for tech/data) and premium serif (for aspirational wellness quotes).
- Palette: A cohesive blend of Medical White, Pure Blue, Deep Ocean gradients, Soft Teal, and Nature Green.

2. Page Structure & Hubs:
- HERO SECTION: A breathtaking visual intersection of a laboratory-clean environment and pristine nature. Headline: Medical-Grade Trust meets Natures Purity". Subheadline: "Premium water filtration that protects your family, optimizes your health, and saves the planet." Primary CTA: "Analyze Your Water".

- HUB 1: SAFETY (The Protector)
  - Mood: Relief & Security. Colors: Medical White, Pure Blue.
  - Content: A "Hidden Toxins" warning section (PFAS, Microplastics, Heavy Metals). 
  - Component: A high-contrast comparison table (Tap Water vs. Bottled vs. EcovodaLife). TrustBar with medical certifications.

- HUB 2: WELLNESS (The Optimizer)
  - Mood: Desire & Vitality. Colors: Soft Teal to Light Blue organic gradients.
  - Content: Focus on biohacking, cellular hydration, and skin/hair health. 
  - Component: Aesthetic cards with serif quotes and imagery of mineral crystals.

- HUB 3: ECO (The Sustainer)
  - Mood: Empowerment & Alignment. Colors: Nature-integrated, soft green accents, airy whitespace.
  - Content: Zero-Waste movement, Plastic-Free living.
  - Component: A visual counter of "Plastic Bottles Saved" and a gallery of sustainable home habits.

- HUB 4: TECHNOLOGY (The Solver)
  - Mood: Technical Trust & Precision. Colors: Deep Ocean gradients.
  - Content: The "Engine" of the brand.
  - Component: Blueprint-style technical schematics/diagrams showing RO (Reverse Osmosis), UV Sterilization, Carbon, and Ion Exchange. Highlight "Smart IoT Sensors" for real-time TDS/pH monitoring.

- HUB 5: PRACTICALITY (The Pragmatist)
  - Mood: Clarity & Confidence. Colors: Structured grids, utility-focused.
  - Content: Logic and ROI.
  - Component: An interactive "ROI Calculator" preview (Bottled water cost vs. Home System) and a "Symptom Checker" (e.g., "Limescale in kettle" -> "Solution: Ion Exchange").

Final Footer: Sophisticated, minimal, with links to the 5 Knowledge Hubs.

Ensure smooth visual transitions between the "moods" of each hub while maintaining a singular luxury brand identity.
