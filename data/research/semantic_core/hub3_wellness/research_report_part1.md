# Semantic Research Report: Hub 3 (Wellness) - Part 1

## Overview
This report covers the first phase of semantic research for the Wellness hub (ecovodalife), focusing on high-level keyword mapping, mid-tail expansion across three languages (EN, RU, SR), and the identification of core search intents.

## 1. High-Level Keyword Mapping
The wellness strategy for pure water is built around five core pillars. Each pillar addresses a specific user need, from basic health to aesthetic goals.

| Main Topic | Core Value Proposition | Primary User Driver |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Health Benefits** | Purity as the foundation of physiological health | Desire for overall wellness & disease prevention |
| **Detox** | Pure water as a catalyst for toxin removal | Desire for "reset", weight loss, or purity |
| **Hydration** | Optimal cellular water balance | Performance, energy levels, and cognitive function |
| **Longevity** | Long-term impact of water quality on aging | Aspirational living and life extension |
| **Skin/Hair Health** | External beauty reflected by internal purity | Aesthetic improvement and dermatological health |

## 2. Search Intent Identification
We have categorized the identified keywords into three primary intent types:

- **Informational:** Users seeking knowledge about the benefits of pure water (e.g., "Why is purified water better than tap?").
- **Educational:** Users looking for "how-to" guides or optimal standards (e.g., "How much water should I drink for detox?").
- **Solution-oriented:** Users looking for a remedy to a specific problem (e.g., "How to fix skin elasticity with hydration").

## 3. Mid-Tail Expansion Strategy
The keyword expansion was performed for English (EN), Russian (RU), and Serbian (SR) to capture regional market nuances.

### Linguistic Nuances Observed:
- **English (EN):** High focus on "cellular hydration" and "anti-aging" trends.
- **Russian (RU):** Strong interest in "detox" (детокс) and the contrast between "purified" (очищенная) and "tap" (водопроводная) water.
- **Serbian (SR):** Emphasis on "benefiti" (benefits) and "čistoća" (purity), with a growing trend towards "anti-aging" and "detoksikacija".

## 4. Next Steps (Part 2)
The subsequent phase of research will involve:
- Long-tail keyword mining.
- Competitor semantic gap analysis.
- Mapping keywords to specific content clusters (Articles, Guides, Product Pages).
