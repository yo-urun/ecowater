## 2. Main Content Gaps (RU Market)

Based on a comparative analysis of global trends vs. RU wellness landscape:

1. **Sustainable Biohacking (Эко-биохакинг):** Most RU biohacking content is focused on supplements and "pill-popping". There is a gap in "natural" or "sustainable" biohacking (focus on environment, light, water, and ancestral habits).
2. **Local Longevity Context:** Lack of longevity guides specifically tailored to the Russian climate (e.g., combating seasonal affective disorder, Vitamin D management, winter nutrition).
3. **Water-Centric Wellness:** While "вода" is in the brand name, few RU sites effectively link water chemistry/structure to overall longevity and systemic health in a scientifically backed way.
4. **Mental Fitness for Urbanites:** High demand for "stress-management" but a gap in "emotional fitness" — proactive training of the mind rather than just reactive stress relief.

## 3. Summary of Findings
The "Wellness" hub for ecovodalife should pivot from generic "health tips" to a synthesis of **Eco-Living + Longevity + Precision Hydration**. This unique positioning will allow the site to capture long-tail traffic from both the "eco" community and the "biohacking" community.
