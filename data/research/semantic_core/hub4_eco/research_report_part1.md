# Semantic Research Report: Hub 4 - Sustainable Living (ecovodalife) - Part 1

## 1. Introduction
This document represents the first phase of the semantic research for Hub 4, focusing on Sustainable Living. The goal of this phase is to establish a high-level keyword map, expand these into mid-tail keywords across three target languages (English, Russian, and Serbian), and identify the primary search intents associated with each topic.

## 2. High-Level Keyword Mapping
The semantic core of Hub 4 is divided into four primary pillars:

### Pillar 1: Zero-Waste
- **Core Concept:** Minimizing the amount of waste produced by a household or individual.
- **Focus Areas:** Waste reduction, composting, refill stations, circular economy.
- **Primary Intent:** Informational (How-to) $\to$ Commercial (Products).

### Pillar 2: Plastic-Free
- **Core Concept:** Eliminating single-use plastics and transitioning to sustainable materials.
- **Focus Areas:** Alternatives to plastic packaging, plastic-free personal care, ocean plastic awareness.
- **Primary Intent:** Commercial Investigation $\to$ Transactional (Buying alternatives).

### Pillar 3: Eco-friendly Home
- **Core Concept:** Creating a living space that minimizes environmental impact.
- **Focus Areas:** Energy efficiency, sustainable materials, non-toxic cleaning, eco-decor.
- **Primary Intent:** Commercial (Products) $\to$ Informational (Optimization tips).

### Pillar 4: Carbon Footprint
- **Core Concept:** Measuring and reducing the total greenhouse gas emissions caused by an individual.
- **Focus Areas:** Carbon calculators, transportation alternatives, energy saving, carbon offsetting.
- **Primary Intent:** Informational (Education) $\to$ Transactional (Offsetting services).

## 3. Intent Analysis
| Topic | Search Intent | User Motivation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Zero-Waste** | Informational $\to$ Commercial | "I want to know how to reduce waste and what products help me do it." |
| **Plastic-Free** | Commercial $\to$ Transactional | "I am looking for a specific alternative to a plastic item I currently use." |
| **Eco-friendly Home** | Commercial $\to$ Informational | "I want to buy eco-products and learn how to make my home more sustainable." |
| **Carbon Footprint** | Informational $\to$ Transactional | "I want to understand my impact on the planet and find ways to neutralize it." |

## 4. Methodology
- **Language Expansion:** Keywords were expanded into RU, EN, and SR using a combination of semantic translation and localized web search verification to ensure natural phrasing.
- **Scope:** This part focused exclusively on head and mid-tail keywords to establish the foundation of the semantic core. Long-tail queries and competitor-specific keywords are reserved for Part 2.
